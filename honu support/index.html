Specialised Services

Our Premium Treatments

🩹

Kinesiology Taping

Elastic therapeutic taping to support muscles, reduce swelling, and enhance proprioception during activity.

💉

Dry Needling

Precision needle insertion into myofascial trigger points to release tension, reduce pain, and restore function.

🫙

Cupping Therapy

Suction-based decompression therapy to improve blood flow, relieve muscle tightness, and accelerate recovery.

🏥

Myofascial Release

Sustained manual pressure to release fascial restrictions, restoring tissue mobility and reducing chronic pain.

🩺

IASTM

Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization – using specialised tools to break down scar tissue and fascial adhesions.
